Tables in the Entomological Database:
1. species:
    - Data Table
    Contains information about a species of arthropod. 
2. specimens:
    - Data Table
    Contains information about an individual animal in the keeping of the Entomological
    Archive
3. clients:
    - Data Table
    Contains information about people who have made requests of and/or transactions with
    the Entomological Archive
4. requests: 
    - Data Table
    Contains information about requests to checkout specimens from the archive submitted
    to the archive
